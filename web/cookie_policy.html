What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us recognize you and remember your preferences.

How we use cookies:

  1. Authentication: We use cookies to keep you signed in and manage your session.
  2. Preferences: Cookies help us remember your settings and preferences.
  3. Third-party services: Some features rely on third-party services (e.g., Google Sign-In, Firebase) that may use their own cookies.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Necessary cookies: Essential for the app's basic functions.
  • Preference cookies: Store your settings and choices.
  • Third-party cookies: Used by external services integrated into our app.

Managing cookies:

You can manage cookie preferences through your device settings. However, disabling certain cookies may limit app functionality.
